About M.V. Bay Cruise I Bay Cruise

MV Bay Curise is a modern cruise ship in Bangladesh. It goes St. Martin's Island directly from Teknaf. Farhan Tourism, a Cox's Bazar based company, has added this state-of-the-art cruise ship for tourists on the St. Martin route from Teknaf. After Sukanta Babu, it is another life in the travel industry of Bangladesh.

The luxury MV Bay Cruise ship starts it's journey on the St. Martin route from Teknaf to provide a new experience of traveling on international standard luxury cruise ships in Bangladesh.
